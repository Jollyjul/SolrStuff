We have been requested to correct the statement made in our issue of
Aug. 1, 1911, wherein mention is made of Mirza. Ali Kuli Khan, Charge
de'Aifaires of Persia, of Washington, D. O., being honorary president of the
Persian-American Educational Society. We have been informed that his
acceptance of this title depends upon the Wishes of his government, from
Whom consent has not yet been received.
